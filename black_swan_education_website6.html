The approach

A swan's stillness is a solved problem, not a talent.

Every session starts from where the student actually is, not where the syllabus assumes they are. We build backward from that gap.

Diagnose

Find the real gap

A proper first assessment, not a worksheet. We find the exact concept where understanding broke down, often earlier than the current topic suggests.

Rebuild

Fix the foundations

We close the gap before racing ahead. Skipping this step is why tutoring so often stalls at "better, but still shaky."

Prove it

Perform under pressure

Timed papers, cold questions, real exam conditions. Confidence that hasn't been tested isn't confidence yet.
